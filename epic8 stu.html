Final Exam Schedule

May 15, 2023

The final examination schedule for the Spring 2023 semester has been published. Please check the dates and times for your courses carefully.

Important Information:

  • All exams will be conducted in person at the designated locations
  • Students must bring their student ID cards
  • Exams will follow the regular class schedule unless otherwise noted
  • Special accommodations must be arranged at least one week in advance

If you have any conflicts with your exam schedule, please contact the Registrar's Office immediately. The deadline for resolving schedule conflicts is May 22nd.

Good luck with your preparations!
